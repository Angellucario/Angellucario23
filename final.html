How To Play

In Tetris, your goal is to score points by making complete horizontal lines with different shaped blocks that fall from the top of the screen. You start the game by clicking the "Start Game" button. Use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to move the blocks side to side. Press the down arrow key to make the blocks fall faster. When you fill up a whole line with blocks, it disappears and you get points. The game keeps going with more blocks falling faster until there's no more room for new blocks.

  • Right key moves block Left
  • Left key moves block Left
  • Down key makes blocks move down quicker
  • Make a row and get 100 point
  • Once a block touches the top, you lose!
